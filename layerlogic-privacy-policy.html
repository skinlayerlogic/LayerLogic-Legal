LayerLogic Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 12, 2026

LayerLogic ("we," "us," or "our") is a mobile application that helps you build, track, and optimize your skincare routine. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have regarding your data.

By using LayerLogic, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

1. Information We Collect

Data Type Details Purpose
Email Address Provided during account creation Authentication, account recovery, and service communications
Skin Profile Skin type, skin concerns, skin goals Personalizing your skincare routine recommendations
Product Data Product names, barcode scan results, product images Building and managing your product library and routine
Camera Access Used for barcode scanning and optional selfie-based skin analysis Identifying products and analyzing skin condition
Photos & Images Product photos you upload Visual product identification and routine tracking

We do not collect precise geolocation data, financial information, or contacts from your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Personalize skincare routine recommendations based on your skin profile
  • Look up product information from barcode scans
  • Store and display the product images you upload
  • Provide selfie-based skin analysis features (when you opt in)
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Communicate important service updates
