LayerLogic Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 12, 2026

LayerLogic ("we," "us," or "our") is a mobile application that helps you build, track, and optimize your skincare routine. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have regarding your data.

By using LayerLogic, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

1. Information We Collect

Data Type Details Purpose
Email Address Provided during account creation Authentication, account recovery, and service communications
Skin Profile Skin type, skin concerns, skin goals Personalizing your skincare routine recommendations
Product Data Product names, barcode scan results, product images Building and managing your product library and routine
Camera Access Used for barcode scanning and optional selfie-based skin analysis Identifying products and analyzing skin condition
Photos & Images Product photos you upload Visual product identification and routine tracking

We do not collect precise geolocation data, financial information, or contacts from your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Personalize skincare routine recommendations based on your skin profile
  • Look up product information from barcode scans
  • Store and display the product images you upload
  • Provide selfie-based skin analysis features (when you opt in)
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Communicate important service updates

3. Third-Party Services

LayerLogic relies on the following third-party services to operate. Each service receives only the minimum data necessary to perform its function:

Service Function Data Shared
Created.app Backend infrastructure and database Account data, skin profile, product data
Uploadcare Image hosting and storage Product photos and images you upload
Open Food Facts API Product barcode lookups Barcode numbers (no personal data)

These services operate under their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them. We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your account is secured using JSON Web Tokens (JWT), which are stored locally on your device using secure, encrypted storage (SecureStore). We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data during transmission and storage.

While no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, we take reasonable steps to safeguard your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal information from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as resolving disputes or enforcing our terms).

Images stored via Uploadcare will be deleted in accordance with our data removal process following account deletion.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your account and associated personal data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for optional features like camera access or skin analysis at any time through your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address listed below. We will respond to requests within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

LayerLogic is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will take immediate steps to delete that information. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you through the app or via email. The "Effective Date" at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of LayerLogic after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
